# **App Name**: GanaderoSync Global

## Core Features:

- Global Software Presentation: Present GanaderoSync to a global audience, showcasing its benefits for feedlot management.
- Premium Design: Use premium, elegant, and avant-garde design to convey high quality and modernity.
- Worldwide Selling: Enable online purchase and subscription for global users.
- AI-Powered Trend Analysis: Employ AI to analyze feedlot data and predict trends related to weight gain, mortality, and stock valuation. The LLM should act as a tool deciding when this info is relevant to show
- Dashboard Preview: Offer potential customers a glimpse of the dashboard’s layout and real-time monitoring capabilities.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Teal (#008080) to represent trust and sophistication, while still reflecting the brand’s digital presence.
- Background color: Off-white (#F0F8FF) to offer a clean, modern, and user-friendly experience.
- Accent color: Steel Blue (#4682B4) for key CTAs and highlights to convey premium quality and modernity.
- Headline font: 'Poppins', a geometric sans-serif with a precise, contemporary feel.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif that combines a modern look and approachability.
- Crisp, modern icons related to data management, animal health, and financial metrics.
- Clean, well-organized layout with a focus on data visualization. Use modern chart designs to display key metrics.
